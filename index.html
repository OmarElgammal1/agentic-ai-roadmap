Step 5 Even for Experts

Level Up Your Toolkit

These frameworks bring unique capabilities you won't find elsewhere. They're not just for beginners.

Important Note

Pydantic AI and Agno are worth learning even if you're already experienced with agentic AI. They offer type safety, performance, and developer ergonomics that complement any existing stack.

Pydantic AI
Free
ai.pydantic.dev — Official Documentation
Type-safe structured outputs, dependency injection, auto self-correction, and 30+ model providers. The "FastAPI feeling" for agent development.
→

Free

Lightweight, performance-first framework. Built-in tools, knowledge bases, memory, and multimodal I/O. Claims 10,000× faster agent creation.
